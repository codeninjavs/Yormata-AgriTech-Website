Our Services

What We Do

We offer a range of services to support farmers, agribusinesses, and communities in Kono District, Sierra Leone. From precision farming to training programs, our goal is to enhance productivity and sustainability.

Poultry Farm

Our poultry division produces healthy, locally grown chickens and eggs under the highest hygiene and nutritional standards. From hatchlings to full-grown broilers and layers, we supply to homes, markets, and retailers across Sierra Leone.

Vegetable Production Department

We cultivate a wide range of fresh, chemical-free vegetables using precision agriculture and eco-friendly methods. Our produce includes cabbage, tomatoes, lettuce, peppers, okra, and more.

Animal Feed Production

To support livestock farmers, we produce high-quality, nutrient-rich animal feed tailored for poultry, cattle, goats, and sheep. Our feeds are locally sourced, affordable, and designed to boost animal health and productivity.

Rice Production

We are committed to reducing rice import dependency by cultivating high-yield, climate-resilient rice varieties across multiple regions in Sierra Leone. Our rice fields are fully mechanized to ensure large-scale and timely harvests.

How We Work

How We Do Agricultural Work

At Yormatah AgriTech Farm, we are committed to sustainable and innovative agricultural practices that empower local farmers and communities. Our approach combines traditional farming wisdom with modern technology to create a resilient agricultural ecosystem.

01

Sustainable Land Conversion

We transform degraded land into productive agricultural zones through reforestation, soil restoration, and sustainable farming practices.

02

Organic Farming Techniques

We promote organic farming practices that enhance soil health, increase biodiversity, and reduce chemical inputs.

03

Integrated Farming System

We implement integrated farming systems that combine crops, livestock, and aquaculture to create a sustainable and self-sufficient agricultural ecosystem.

04

Training & Community Involvement

We provide training programs and workshops to empower local farmers with the skills and knowledge needed for sustainable agriculture.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Yormatah AgriTech Farm do?

We are an agricultural technology company in Sierra Leone focused on sustainable farming. Our services include poultry farming, vegetable production, rice cultivation, hatchery services, and animal feed production all powered by modern techniques and community engagement.

Can farmers partner or get training from you?

Absolutely. We work closely with local farmers, offering training, resources, and cooperative partnerships. If you're interested in joining our farmer empowerment program, please reach out to our team.

Are your products organic?

Yes. We prioritize organic farming practices and ensure that our products are free from synthetic chemicals. Visit our About or Contact page for more info.

How can I purchase your products?

You can purchase our products directly from our farm or through local markets. For bulk orders or inquiries, please contact us via our Contact page.

Can I invest or collaborate with your company?

Yes. We are open to partnerships, investments, and collaborations with NGOs, businesses, and government agencies interested in sustainable agriculture and rural development.

Where are your farms located?

Our farms are primarily located in Kono District, Sierra Leone. We also have partnerships with local farmers across the country to promote sustainable agriculture.
